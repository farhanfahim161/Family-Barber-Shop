About Gino's

Welcome to Gino's Family Barber Shop, your neighborhood destination for quality haircuts and traditional barbering in Mantua, New Jersey.

We're a family-run establishment that believes in honest work, fair prices, and treating every customer like family. With two experienced barbers on staff, you'll never wait long for a great cut.

Located conveniently across from Tractor Supply on Bridgeton Pike, we're here to serve the community with the kind of classic barbershop experience that never goes out of style.
